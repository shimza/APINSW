Fuel Pricing and Availability is a part of all of our lives.

Fuel pricing, location and availability is all communicated through the NSW Fuel API.

Conceptual Usages of Fuel Pricing Data

Fuel Pricing on mobile maps, with real time directions and cost calculation to the Fuel Station.

Instructions to Fleet Drivers where to fill up this shift, based on cheapest available on Route.

Combine insurance, car servicing and fuel pricing into a Fused Discount offer.

Understand patterns and implications in real time of package delivery cost variability, in line with fuel costs.

Determine the cheapest time of the week for your fleet to fill up, alert them via SMS.

The Fuel Data

This API will accept two calls an hour to receive updates.

If you require more frequent data updates call us and we can discuss pricing options.
